About Me

My name is Alex and I'm a senior at UC Berkeley studying computer science. My interests are centered around machine learning and deep learning, particularly in their applications in natural language processing and computer vision.

When I'm not building neural networks, I produce electronic dance music and hip hop beats. My dream is to find ways to integrate machine learning to solve problems in audio synthesis, and in general to find ways to streamline the music production process.

Other interests of mine include reading (particularly in psychology), playing basketball, hiking and jamming on the guitar.
